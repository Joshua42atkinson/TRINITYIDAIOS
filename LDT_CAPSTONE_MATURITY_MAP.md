# LDT Capstone Maturity Map

This document evaluates the maturity of the **Trinity ID AI OS** specifically for the Purdue University Learning Design and Technology (LDT) Capstone submission. It maps the portfolio artifacts, software architecture, and pedagogical frameworks against established instructional design standards.

## 1. Pedagogical Integration (Cognitive & Self-Determination Theory)

The core architecture of Trinity represents a structural fusion of Cognitive Load Theory (CLT) and Self-Determination Theory (SDT). 

- **Student-Driven Pacing**: The integration of **Professor Pete** acting as a Socratic Mentor ensures the system never dictates the speed of learning. The *student* drives the pace. 
- **Cognitive Thermodynamics**: When intrinsic/extraneous load threatens to overwhelm the learner (represented by "Cargo" and "Friction"), the system's "Ghost Train" dynamic safely pauses the environment, adhering to Sweller's Cognitive Load Theory.
- **Autonomy & Competence**: Through the Dual-Persona Socratic Squeeze, the user achieves mastery incrementally, directly satisfying Deci & Ryan’s requirements for intrinsic motivation.

## 2. Institutional Standards Alignment

This artifact demonstrates full compliance with key adult learning frameworks, elevating it from a "Super Textbook" into a **Full-Spectrum Educational Tool**.

### IBSTPI (International Board of Standards for Training, Performance and Instruction)
- **Professional Foundations**: The system's Memory-Safe Rust backend natively enforces FERPA/COPPA compliance via a strictly local-first processing environment. (Artifact: The Fancy Bible)
- **Planning & Analysis**: The architectural split between the Great Recycler and Programmer Pete demonstrates forensic environmental analysis of the learner's needs before compilation. (Artifact: Field Manual)
- **Design & Development**: The rigorous ADDIECRAPEYE constraint loop prevents AI hallucination and ensures all generated educational content is aligned with measurable objectives. (Artifact: Professor Programmer)

### Quality Matters (QM) Higher Education Rubric
- **Course Overview & Introduction**: Trinity's "Character Sheet" acts as a transparent, user-controlled contract setting immediate expectations and prerequisites.
- **Learner Support & Interaction**: The Socratic Multilingual Engine (Mistral 4 Small) ensures continuous 24/7 instructional support, lowering the affective filter and promoting safe drafting.

### AECT (Association for Educational Communications and Technology)
- **Creating & Utilizing**: Utilization of local LLMs and Bevy ECS to create kinetic, dynamic learning ecosystems that adapt in real-time.
- **Evaluating & Managing**: Implementing the VAAM engine to objectively track vocabulary acquisition and comprehension prior to advancing content delivery.

The capstone portfolio is stabilized by four definitive pillars:
