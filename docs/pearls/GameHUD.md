# Game HUD (Gamification)
## PEARL Alignment Document

This document defines the quality gates for the **Game HUD** UI interface (`GameHUD.jsx`), functioning as the persistent right-rail tracking system on the Iron Road. It follows the **EYE** (Evaluation, Yield, Evolve) stage of the ADDIECRAPEYE lifecycle.

### P - Perspective
*Who is the user in this view, and what is their immediate goal?*
- **User State (Cognitive Load: Low):** The user is adopting the "Player Protagonist" persona, frequently glancing at their inventory or stat sheet.
- **Goal:** To monitor their LitRPG resource economy (Coal, Steam, XP), review their "Session Zero" identity anchors, and inspect their "Bestiary" of tamed vocabulary without losing context of the chat.

### E - Engineering
*What backend services, APIs, or components drive this view?*
- **Component:** `GameHUD.jsx` aggregates `PearlCard`, `TrainStatus`, and `CreepCard` subcomponents.
- **Backend Bindings (Rust):**
  - **`crate::character_sheet::get_character`**: Polled regularly to sync XP, Coal, and Steam resources visually with the UI.
  - **`crate::quests::get_active_quest`**: Hydrates the `PearlCard` and `Session Zero` identity anchors from the `IronRoadState`.
  - **`crate::quests::get_bestiary`** / **`crate::agent::narrative_gen`**: Generates narrative records via `POST /api/narrative/generate` inside the `BookSection`.
- **Dependencies:** 
  - Integrates direct data from the top-level `useBestiary` and `useQuest` hooks (bound via Server-Sent Events from `crate::main::sse_handler`).

### A - Aesthetic
*What is the visual language, color scheme, and mood?*
- **Palette/Mood:** Tactile, RPG "Inventory Screen" aesthetic. Relies on `.card` containers with slate backgrounds and distinct header labels. System health badges (CowCatcher, EdgeGuard) use green/warning indicators to simulate dashboard instrumentation on a locomotive.
- **Key Elements:** 
  - Stat row displaying `⚡ XP`, `🪨 Coal`, and `💨 Steam`.
  - The Sacred Circuitry matrix mapping the four phase domains (Scope, Build, Listen, Ship) dynamically to Iron Road concepts.
  - The "Book of the Bible" renders with `'Crimson Text', serif` font styling to contrast the technical UI with high-fantasy lore.

### R - Research
*What data informs this design? How does it align with the Fancy Bible?*
- **The Mirror Mechanic:** Directly fulfills the "Mirror Mechanic", creating an instantaneous reflection of the backend `crate::quests::GameState`. 
- **Session Zero Sync:** Captures the "Session Zero" user definitions (Experience, Audience, Vision) tracked in the global `AppState` and immediately projects them onto the screen as tangible artifacts. This explicitly transparentizes how `crate::agent` grounds its generation behaviors, reinforcing user agency.

### L - Layout (C.R.A.P. Methodology)
*How does this view use Contrast, Repetition, Alignment, and Proximity?*
- **Contrast:** Warning indicators (CowCatcher / EdgeGuard health badges) use stark green/red fills to instantly highlight backend systemic threats, contrasting sharply against the passive gray slate of the card backgrounds.
- **Repetition:** Bestiary items (`CreepCard` components) utilize identical tight 1:1 aspect ratios, generating a satisfying "Trading Card Game" collection grid as the user unlocks more concepts.
- **Alignment:** Acts as a persistent, rigid right-rail sidebar. It visually aligns as the permanent companion to the `PhaseWorkspace`, stacking vertically into three semantic blocks (Journey -> Identity -> Collection).
- **Proximity:** The core resource stats (`⚡ XP`, `🪨 Coal`, `💨 Steam`) are pinned tightly into a single flex-row at the absolute top, ensuring the user's "vitals" are always immediately accessible at a glance.
