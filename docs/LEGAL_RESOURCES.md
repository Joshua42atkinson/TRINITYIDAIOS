# Legal & Ownership Resource Document
## TRINITY ID AI OS — Purdue University Partnership

### 1. Core Licensing: Apache License 2.0
Trinity is released under the **Apache License, Version 2.0**. This is a permissive free software license that provides significant benefits for both the project and its users:

- **Freedom to Use**: Users can run the software for any purpose, including commercial education or corporate training.
- **Freedom to Modify**: Developers (including Purdue students/staff) can fork and improve the system.
- **Freedom to Distribute**: The software can be distributed freely, provided the original license and copyright notices are preserved.
- **Patent Grant**: The license includes an explicit grant of patent rights from contributors to users, protecting against patent "trolls."
- **Liability Protection**: The software is provided "as is," protecting the original developer (Joshua) and Purdue from liability claims.

### 2. Ownership of AI-Generated Content
A critical pillar of Trinity is that the **USER retains full ownership** of all educational assets created with the system.

- **Non-Copyrightability of Raw AI Output**: Current US legal standards (as of 2024-2026) generally hold that raw AI output cannot be copyrighted.
- **Human-in-the-Loop Ownership**: Trinity's "Meaning Making" process (VAAM + ADDIECRAPEYE) ensures that every AI-generated asset is chosen, refined, and contextualized by a human Subject Matter Expert. This human creative input provides the legal basis for the User to claim copyright over the final educational "work" or "game."
- **Purdue Protection**: By using an Apache 2.0 license, Purdue University is protected from claims regarding the output generated by users of the platform.

### 3. Nonprofit Foundation Model
To ensure the long-term maintenance and classroom safety of Trinity, a **Trinity ID AI OS Foundation** (Nonprofit) is proposed:

- **Mission**: Maintaining the "Iron Road" as a free, offline-first tool for K-12 educators.
- **Grant Funding**: The nonprofit can accept grants (NSF, Department of Education) to develop new "Sacred Circuitry" patterns and academic datasets.
- **Community Managed**: Governance can be shared between the original developer, Purdue LDT faculty, and the educator community.
- **Free for All**: The foundation guarantees that the core system remains open-source and free, preventing corporate capture.

### 4. Alignment & Safety Resources
Trinity includes built-in quality gates to ensure academic and branding safety:

- **Quality Matters (QM) Alignment**: Automated checks for instructional design quality.
- **VAAM Semantic Search**: Ensuring assets are meaningful and aligned with pedagogical intent.
- **Research Pillar (R in ART)**: Automated "duplication checks" to prevent unintentional infringement on existing IP.
- **Offline First**: All data remains local to the classroom server (EVO X2), ensuring student privacy and compliance with data protection laws (FERPA/GDPR).
