## 🎯 **ADDIE OVERVIEW**

### **📋 What is ADDIE?**
ADDIE is a systematic instructional design framework consisting of five phases:
- **Analysis**: Conduct needs assessment and audience analysis
- **Design**: Create learning objectives and assessment strategies
- **Development**: Develop content and learning materials
- **Implementation**: Deploy course and setup support systems
- **Evaluation**: Assess effectiveness and recommend improvements

### **🤖 Trinity's Role**
Trinity enhances ADDIE workflows through:
- **AI-Powered Analysis**: Automated needs assessment and SME interviews
- **Professional Standards**: IBSTPI, ATD, AECT compliance
- **Quality Assurance**: Quality Matters rubric automation
- **Content Generation**: Multi-modal content creation
- **Evaluation Analytics**: Learning effectiveness measurement
