## What Each Competitor Does That Trinity Doesn't (Yet)

### Antigravity IDE (Google)
- **Cloud-scale models**: Access to Gemini/Claude at datacenter speed — Trinity is edge-bound
- **Multi-file refactoring**: Thousands of files, cross-repo awareness
- **Auto-review**: CI/CD integration, PR reviews
- **Trinity's advantage**: Privacy, gamification, ID methodology, no subscription

### Canvas LMS
- **Institutional deployment**: SSO, gradebook, enrollment, accreditation
- **SCORM/LTI**: Industry-standard course packaging
- **Student analytics**: Learning analytics, grade tracking
- **Trinity's advantage**: AI-powered design (Canvas has no AI design), local ownership

### Adobe Creative Cloud
- **Professional tools**: 40+ years of Photoshop/Premiere/After Effects maturity
- **Industry standard**: Every design firm uses it
- **Firefly AI**: Cloud-based generative AI with IP indemnification
- **Trinity's advantage**: ID framework (Adobe doesn't teach you design), local AI

### Articulate 360
- **Best-in-class eLearning**: Rise 360 is the gold standard for rapid eLearning
- **Review 360**: Stakeholder review workflow
- **Content Library**: Millions of stock assets, templates, characters
- **SCORM/xAPI**: Production-grade LMS packages
- **Trinity's advantage**: Local AI, gamification, price ($0 vs $1,400/yr), customization

### Roblox Studio
- **3D game engine**: Full Lua scripting, physics, multiplayer
- **Distribution**: 70M+ daily active users
- **Monetization**: Revenue sharing for creators
- **Trinity's advantage**: Educational rigor (Roblox has no ID methodology), privacy
